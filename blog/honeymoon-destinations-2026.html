5 Dreamy Honeymoon Destinations for 2026

By Elizabeth Nichols January 20, 2026 7 min read

Your honeymoon is one of the most meaningful trips you'll ever take — and in 2026, couples are seeking something beyond the expected. After years of planning dream honeymoons for our clients, Elizabeth and I have identified five destinations that are trending right now for all the right reasons: breathtaking scenery, unmatched romance, exclusive experiences, and service that makes you feel like the only two people on Earth.

Destination 01

The Maldives — The Gold Standard of Romance

The Maldives remains the undisputed honeymoon capital of the world — and for 2026, the islands have raised the bar even higher. We're seeing our couples choose private island resorts with dedicated butler service, underwater dining experiences, and sunrise paddleboarding over crystal-clear lagoons. The trend this year is booking villa-only resorts where you'll never share a pool or beach with more than a handful of guests.

Resorts like Soneva Fushi, Gili Lankanfushi, and Cheval Blanc Randheli offer jaw-dropping overwater villas with private pools, outdoor showers under the stars, and direct lagoon access. Sunsets here aren't just beautiful — they feel personal, like the island is putting on a show just for you.

Why 2026 is the year to go
  • New ultra-private villa resorts opening on uninhabited atolls
  • New Maldives air connections from the US via Doha and Dubai
  • Best value in the dry season: December–April travel window
  • Destinara can secure complimentary room upgrades & resort credits

Destination 02

Santorini, Greece — Iconic for a Reason

There's a reason Santorini tops every honeymoon list — it genuinely delivers. The caldera views from Oia and Imerovigli are unlike anything else in the world, and the combination of cave hotels carved into volcanic rock, volcanic sand beaches, and world-class Greek cuisine makes every moment feel cinematic. In 2026, we're recommending couples arrive in late April or early October to enjoy the romance without the peak-summer crowds.

The savviest honeymooners are now pairing Santorini with a night or two in Athens (for the Acropolis and the buzzing food scene) and sometimes extending into Mykonos for a contrasting energy. The Greek island-hop honeymoon is one of our most requested itineraries this year.

Destinara's Santorini picks
  • Katikies Hotel — infinity pool with caldera views, adults-only
  • Grace Hotel Santorini — elegant, cliff-side, with Champagne sunsets
  • Private sunset sailing catamaran with onboard dinner
  • Wine tasting tour through Assyrtiko vineyards in Pyrgos

Destination 03

Bali, Indonesia — The Wellness Honeymoon

Bali is having a massive resurgence as a honeymoon destination for 2026, and the couples choosing it are decidedly different from the backpacker crowd of a decade ago. Today's Bali honeymoon means a private villa in Ubud with your own plunge pool surrounded by jungle, couples' Balinese massage and flower-petal bath rituals, sunrise hikes up Mount Batur followed by breakfast in the clouds, and romantic rice-terrace dinners lit by torchlight.

The luxury villa scene in Bali is genuinely world-class — at a fraction of what you'd pay in the Maldives or Santorini. A 7-night Bali honeymoon in a stunning private villa with daily breakfast and a personal driver costs far less than you'd imagine, leaving room in the budget for incredible experiences.

Trending Bali experiences for 2026
  • Private Chef dining experience in your villa garden
  • Couples' healing ceremony with a traditional Balinese healer
  • White-water rafting through the Ayung River gorge
  • Temple tour at Tanah Lot at sunset with a local guide
💡

Advisor Tip from Elizabeth: One of the most overlooked honeymoon upgrades is a "room drop" — a surprise in-room setup arranged secretly through the hotel. Think rose petals, champagne on ice, personalized notes, and floating candles in the bath. We arrange these complimentary for all our honeymooners through our preferred partner properties. It's the detail that makes couples cry (happy tears!) when they walk into their room the first night.

Destination 04

Amalfi Coast, Italy — La Dolce Vita Honeymoon

The Amalfi Coast is consistently one of our top sellers for honeymooners, and 2026 is no exception. What makes it irresistible: dramatically beautiful cliffside scenery, exceptional food and wine, charming villages like Ravello and Positano, and a pace of life that makes you forget time exists. Couples who choose Italy often tell us it was "the most beautiful place we've ever been" — and we tend to agree.

The ideal Amalfi honeymoon combines a few nights in a clifftop villa in Ravello (the quieter, more romantic alternative to Positano), a private boat day cruising the coastline and stopping at hidden sea caves, and a day trip to Pompeii for couples who want a touch of history. Pair it with a night or two in Rome before or after, and you have one of the great honeymoon itineraries in the world.

Must-do experiences
  • Private boat charter along the coast with onboard aperitivo
  • Limoncello making class in a hillside lemon grove
  • Dinner at La Sponda, Positano — candles everywhere, unforgettable
  • Sunrise walk along the Path of the Gods above the clouds

Destination 05

St. Lucia — The Caribbean's Most Romantic Island

Among Caribbean islands, St. Lucia stands apart for honeymooners — it's dramatic, lush, and inherently romantic in a way that beach-flat islands simply aren't. The Piton volcanic peaks rising from the sea, the rainforest zip-lines, the muddy sulphur springs, and the incredible dive sites make it feel adventurous and exotic even within a 4-hour flight from the East Coast. For 2026, St. Lucia has become our go-to recommendation for couples who want the Caribbean but want more.

Jade Mountain Resort — with its open-fourth-wall "Sanctuary" suites overlooking the Pitons — is genuinely one of the most spectacular hotel experiences on the planet. Sugar Beach and Anse Chastanet are equally spectacular alternatives. All three offer honeymooner packages with dedicated butler service and access to incredible marine reserves just offshore.

Why couples love St. Lucia in 2026
  • Direct flights from many East Coast hubs (under 4 hours)
  • UNESCO Biosphere Reserve — pristine and protected nature
  • Some of the best scuba diving in the Caribbean
  • Dramatically less crowded than Aruba, Turks & Caicos, or Cancún
