Expert Packing Tips from Destinara

📚

The 1-2-3 Rule

Pack 1 pair of shoes for walking, 2 for activities, 3 bottoms and 5 tops that all mix and match. You'll be surprised how much less you need.

🧴

Roll, Don't Fold

Rolling clothes instead of folding saves up to 30% more space and reduces wrinkles significantly — especially for casual fabrics.

📦

Carry-On Strategy

Always pack a 48-hour outfit, medications, and valuables in your carry-on. Checked bags can be delayed — your essentials shouldn't be.

⚡

Charge the Night Before

Set every device — phone, camera, power bank, earbuds — to charge the night before departure. Travel day chaos makes this easy to forget.

🛍

Weigh Your Bag

Use a luggage scale before you leave home. Most airlines charge $35-$100 for overweight bags. A quick weigh saves real money.

📋

Photo Your Bag Contents

Before you zip up, photograph the contents of your bag. If luggage is lost, this helps you file an accurate claim with the airline immediately.

💍

Use Packing Cubes

Packing cubes transform chaotic packing into organized sections — one cube per category. Makes airport security and hotel unpacking a breeze.

🏛️

Know the Destination's Culture

Research dress codes before you pack. Many temples, churches, and restaurants require covered shoulders or knees — plan ahead to avoid being turned away.
