Who We Are

Born from a love of exploration

Destinara Travel was founded by Jay and Elizabeth Nichols with a simple belief: travel should be transformative, not transactional. After decades of exploring the world and working in the travel industry, we created Destinara to offer our clients something rare — deeply personal, expertly curated journeys backed by real relationships with the world's finest hotels, cruise lines, and tour operators.

Based in Greensboro, North Carolina, we serve clients across the United States and beyond. Whether you're dreaming of a luxury Mediterranean cruise, a Maldives honeymoon, an African safari, or a multi-generational family adventure, we bring insider access, exclusive perks, and concierge-level care to every single trip.

We're not a booking engine. We're your advocates, your planners, and your partners in exploration — before, during, and long after your journey ends.

Meet the Team

The advisors behind your adventure

With decades of combined experience and a genuine passion for travel, Jay and Elizabeth bring unmatched expertise and personal care to every client relationship.

Elizabeth Nichols

Co-Founder & Travel Advisor

With decades of travel industry experience, Elizabeth brings unmatched expertise in luxury cruises, resort vacations, and destination planning. Her meticulous attention to detail ensures every itinerary is crafted to perfection — from the hotel breakfast to the sunset excursion.

Elizabeth specializes in honeymoons and family vacations, with a particular passion for the Caribbean, Mediterranean, and South Pacific.

Co-Founder & Travel Advisor

Jay's passion for exploration and deep industry connections ensure every client receives insider access and extraordinary experiences. His expertise spans ocean and river cruises, adventure travel, and safari planning across Africa and beyond.

Jay has personally visited over 40 countries and brings that firsthand knowledge to every trip he designs — from a luxury tented safari in Kenya to a Viking river cruise through Europe.
