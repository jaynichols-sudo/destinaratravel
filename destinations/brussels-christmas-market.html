European Christmas Market

Brussels, Belgium

Brussels' Winter Wonders (Plaisirs d'Hiver) is one of Europe's grandest Christmas celebrations, stretching over two kilometers from the iconic Grand Place to Place Sainte-Catherine. The market features over 200 chalets, a massive Ferris wheel, and a spectacular sound and light show on the Grand Place.

What makes Brussels truly special is its world-class food culture elevated to festive heights. Belgian chocolate, fresh waffles, artisanal cheeses, and warming jenever (gin) create a culinary Christmas experience that rivals any in Europe.

Top Experiences

Magical moments at Brussels' Winter Wonders

Grand Place Light Show

Witness the spectacular nightly sound and light show that transforms the gilded facades of the Grand Place into a mesmerizing display of color, music, and holiday storytelling.

Belgian Chocolate Trail

Visit legendary chocolatiers like Pierre Marcolini, Neuhaus, and Mary along the festive streets. Many offer special Advent collections and warm drinking chocolate at market stalls.

Waffles & Winter Treats

Indulge in authentic Belgian waffles â crispy LiÃ¨ge waffles with caramelized sugar or light Brussels waffles topped with chocolate, cream, and fresh berries from market vendors.

Giant Ferris Wheel

Ride the towering Ferris wheel at Place Sainte-Catherine for panoramic views of Brussels' illuminated skyline and the sprawling Christmas market stretching across the city center.

Belgian Beer Culture

Discover Belgium's UNESCO-recognized beer culture at cozy cafes and market stalls. Sample Trappist ales, lambics, and special winter brews alongside hearty Flemish stew.

Artisan Market Stalls

Browse 200+ wooden chalets along the route featuring handcrafted Belgian lace, artisan jewelry, local specialties, and unique gifts from international vendors across Europe.
