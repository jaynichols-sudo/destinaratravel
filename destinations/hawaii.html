Overview

The Aloha State

Hawaii is a chain of eight volcanic islands in the central Pacific Ocean, each offering a distinct personality and experience. From the dramatic Na Pali Coast of Kauai to the fiery lava flows of the Big Island, the world-famous beaches of Maui to the vibrant culture of Oahu, Hawaii is a destination that captivates every sense.

Beyond the stunning beaches and lush rainforests, Hawaii offers a rich tapestry of Polynesian heritage, farm-to-table cuisine, world-class snorkeling, and some of the most spectacular sunrises and sunsets on Earth.

At Destinara, we design Hawaiian getaways that balance relaxation with adventure, connecting you with the authentic spirit of aloha through handpicked resorts, exclusive experiences, and insider knowledge of each island.

Top Experiences
Unforgettable Hawaii Moments
From volcanic adventures to ocean encounters, Hawaii delivers paradise at every turn.

Na Pali Coast

Experience the jaw-dropping Na Pali Coast of Kauai by helicopter, catamaran, or hiking the Kalalau Trail. Towering sea cliffs draped in emerald green plunge into turquoise waters below.

Snorkeling at Molokini

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Molokini Crater off Maui, home to over 250 species of tropical fish. This volcanic crescent is one of the top snorkeling spots in the world.

Hawaii Volcanoes

Witness the raw power of creation at Hawaii Volcanoes National Park on the Big Island. Walk through lava tubes, see steaming craters, and watch where molten lava meets the sea.

Sunset at Waikiki

Watch the sun dip below the Pacific from the legendary shores of Waikiki Beach. Take a surf lesson, enjoy beachside cocktails, and experience the vibrant energy of Honolulu.

Hawaiian Luau

Immerse yourself in Polynesian culture at an authentic Hawaiian luau. Enjoy traditional kalua pork, hula dancing, fire knife performances, and the warm spirit of aloha.

Road to Hana

Drive the legendary Road to Hana on Maui, winding through 620 curves and 59 bridges past waterfalls, bamboo forests, black sand beaches, and breathtaking ocean vistas.

Travel Tips
Know Before You Go
Insider tips from our Hawaii travel experts to help you plan the perfect island getaway.
🏝

Choose Your Island Wisely

Each Hawaiian island has a distinct personality. Maui is great for couples, the Big Island for adventure, Kauai for nature lovers, and Oahu for first-timers and nightlife.

🚗

Rent a Car

A rental car is essential on most islands to explore beyond the resort area. Book early, especially during peak season, as cars can sell out completely.

🌞

Reef-Safe Sunscreen Only

Hawaii law prohibits sunscreens containing oxybenzone and octinoxate to protect coral reefs. Bring reef-safe mineral sunscreen or buy it locally.

💰

Book Activities in Advance

Popular activities like snorkel tours, helicopter rides, and luaus sell out weeks ahead. We recommend booking at least 3–4 weeks before your trip.

🌊

Respect the Land

Hawaiian culture deeply values the land and ocean. Practice responsible tourism by staying on marked trails, not touching wildlife, and following local guidelines.

💉

Travel Insurance Recommended

While Hawaii is domestic, travel insurance covers trip interruptions from weather events, flight cancellations, and medical emergencies in remote areas.
