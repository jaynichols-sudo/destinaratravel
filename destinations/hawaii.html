Hawaii

Island Paradise — Volcanoes, Waterfalls & Aloha Spirit

Hawaii is America's tropical paradise — eight main islands stretched across 1,500 miles of Pacific Ocean, each with its own personality. Maui's golden beaches and whale-watching season, the Big Island's active lava flows and stargazing from Mauna Kea, Kauai's emerald Na Pali Coast, and Oahu's legendary North Shore surf — Hawaii offers infinite variety wrapped in the warm spirit of aloha.

Destination Highlights

Maui — Valley Isle Perfection

Drive the legendary Road to Hana through 64 miles of waterfalls, bamboo forests, and coastal vistas; watch humpback whales breach offshore; and descend into Haleakala Crater at sunrise.

Big Island — Volcanoes & Astronomy

Stand at the edge of Kilauea Volcano in Hawaii Volcanoes National Park, stargaze from Mauna Kea's summit at 13,796 feet, and snorkel with manta rays off the Kona Coast.

Oahu — Waikiki & North Shore

Surf the world-famous breaks of Pipeline on the North Shore, learn the history of Pearl Harbor, hike to the Diamond Head summit, and experience the vibrant culture of Honolulu.

Kauai — The Garden Isle

Explore the Na Pali Coast by sea kayak, helicopter, or hiking trail; visit Waimea Canyon — the Grand Canyon of the Pacific; and relax on Hanalei Bay's perfect crescent of sand.

Snorkeling & Marine Life

Swim with Hawaiian green sea turtles at Turtle Town on Maui, explore the Marine Life Conservation District at Hanauma Bay, and dive with spinner dolphins in Kealakekua Bay.

Traditional Hawaiian Culture

Attend a traditional lu'au with hula, imu underground cooking, and fire dancing; explore the Bishop Museum's Hawaiian heritage collections; and learn the art of lei-making.
