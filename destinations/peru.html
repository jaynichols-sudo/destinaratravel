Peru

Ancient Empires & Andean Wonders

Peru is a land of staggering contrasts — from the soaring peaks of the Andes to the depths of the Amazon rainforest, from the ancient stones of Machu Picchu to the colonial grandeur of Cusco. One of the world's great travel destinations, Peru rewards adventurous souls with extraordinary archaeological sites, vibrant indigenous cultures, remarkable biodiversity, and a food scene that has captivated the world.

Destination Highlights

Machu Picchu & Inca Trail

Trek the legendary Inca Trail through cloud forests and mountain passes to arrive at the Sun Gate overlooking the iconic citadel of Machu Picchu at dawn.

Sacred Valley of the Incas

Explore the fertile Urubamba Valley dotted with Inca ruins, traditional markets, and weaving communities nestled between towering Andean peaks.

Cusco Colonial Old Town

Wander the UNESCO-listed streets of Cusco, where Inca stone foundations support Spanish colonial churches in a remarkable layering of civilizations.

Lake Titicaca Floating Islands

Visit the remarkable Uros floating reed islands on the world's highest navigable lake, where indigenous communities have lived for centuries on hand-made totora reed platforms.

Amazon Rainforest Lodge

Venture into the Peruvian Amazon from a remote eco-lodge, spotting exotic wildlife including macaws, caimans, pink river dolphins, and hundreds of bird species.

Lima's World-Class Food Scene

Experience Lima's legendary culinary scene — from ceviche and tiradito at legendary restaurants to street food markets that have made Peru a global gastronomy destination.
