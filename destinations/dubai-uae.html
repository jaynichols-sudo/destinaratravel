Overview
div>

Where the Future Meets Arabian Tradition

Dubai is a city of superlatives, home to the world's tallest building, largest shopping mall, and most ambitious architectural visions. Rising from the Arabian Desert in just a few decades, this glittering metropolis on the Persian Gulf has transformed itself into one of the world's most exciting destinations.

Yet beyond the jaw-dropping skyline and luxury shopping, Dubai offers surprising depth. Explore the atmospheric lanes of the historic Al Fahidi district, haggle for spices and gold in traditional souks, and discover a thriving arts scene in the converted warehouses of Alserkal Avenue.

The desert surrounding Dubai is an adventure playground in its own right, offering dune bashing safaris, camel treks, hot air balloon rides, and overnight Bedouin-style camps under a canopy of stars. Combined with world-class dining, pristine beaches, and warm hospitality, Dubai delivers an unforgettable experience.

Must-Do Activities
div>

Top Experiences

Burj Khalifa at Sunset

Ascend the world's tallest building for breathtaking panoramic views from the observation decks as the sun sets over the desert and the city lights begin to sparkle below.

div>
div>

Desert Safari Adventure

Race over golden sand dunes in a 4x4, try sandboarding, ride camels at sunset, and feast on Arabian cuisine under the stars at a traditional Bedouin-style camp.

div>
div>

Dubai Marina & Palm Jumeirah

Cruise along the stunning Dubai Marina skyline by yacht or dhow, then explore the iconic Palm Jumeirah island with its luxury resorts and pristine beaches.

div>
div>

Historic Souks & Creek

Cross the Dubai Creek by traditional abra boat and wander through the glittering Gold Souk and aromatic Spice Souk in the atmospheric Deira district.

div>
div>

Dubai Fountain Show

Watch the mesmerizing choreographed water, light, and music performance at the Dubai Fountain, the world's largest choreographed fountain system at the base of the Burj Khalifa.

div>
div>

Hot Air Balloon Over Desert

Float silently over the Arabian Desert at dawn in a hot air balloon, watching the sunrise paint the endless dunes gold while spotting camels and gazelles below.

Best Time to Visit

Recommended
div>

Winter (Nov-Feb)

20-26°C
div>

The perfect time to visit with pleasant temperatures, sunny skies, and outdoor events. Peak tourist season with Dubai Shopping Festival in January and February.

div>
Recommended
div>

Spring (Mar-Apr)

25-33°C
div>

Still comfortable for sightseeing with warming temperatures. Fewer crowds than winter peak, good hotel deals, and perfect beach weather before summer heat arrives.

div>

Summer (May-Sep)

35-45°C
div>

Extremely hot and humid, but indoor attractions are excellent and hotel prices drop dramatically. Dubai Summer Surprises festival offers incredible shopping deals.

div>

Autumn (Oct)

28-35°C
div>

Temperatures begin to cool down from summer extremes. Shoulder season pricing with fewer tourists. Good time for desert activities as evenings become pleasant.

Helpful Advice
div>

Travel Tips

👗
div>

Dress Respectfully

While Dubai is cosmopolitan, dress modestly in public areas, malls, and restaurants. Cover shoulders and knees. Beachwear is fine at the beach and pool areas only.

div>
🚇
div>

Use the Metro

The Dubai Metro is clean, affordable, and connects major attractions. Get a Nol card for easy travel. Taxis and ride-hailing apps are also convenient and reasonably priced.

div>
💰
div>

Free Attractions Exist

Dubai is not all expensive. Enjoy free beach access, the Dubai Fountain shows, mosque visits, heritage walks in Al Fahidi, and stunning public parks throughout the city.

div>
☀
div>

Stay Hydrated

The heat can be intense, especially from May to September. Carry water everywhere, wear sunscreen, and plan outdoor activities for early morning or evening hours.

div>
🍽
div>

Diverse Dining Scene

Dubai's multicultural population creates an incredible food scene. Explore everything from street-side shawarma and Indian restaurants to Michelin-starred fine dining.

div>
📱
div>

Download Key Apps

Download the RTA Dubai app for transport, Careem or Uber for rides, and Zomato for restaurant discoveries. Many attractions offer skip-the-line bookings through their apps.
