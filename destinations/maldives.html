Overview

Paradise on Earth

The Maldives is the ultimate tropical escape, a collection of 1,192 coral islands scattered across the Indian Ocean like jewels on blue silk. With powdery white beaches, impossibly turquoise waters, and some of the most luxurious resorts on the planet, this archipelago defines paradise.

Beneath the surface lies an underwater wonderland of vibrant coral reefs, manta rays, whale sharks, and hundreds of species of tropical fish. The Maldives offers world-class diving and snorkeling that ranks among the best anywhere on earth.

From iconic overwater bungalows and private island resorts to underwater restaurants and spa treatments above the waves, the Maldives delivers an unmatched level of luxury and seclusion for couples, honeymooners, and those seeking the ultimate relaxation.

Must-Do Activities

Top Experiences

Overwater Villa Stay

Experience the ultimate luxury in a private overwater bungalow with glass floors, direct ocean access, and sunrise views over the endless Indian Ocean horizon.

Reef Snorkeling

Glide above stunning house reefs teeming with colorful fish, sea turtles, and graceful rays just steps from your resort in crystal-clear warm waters.

Sunset Dolphin Cruise

Sail across the Indian Ocean at golden hour, watching pods of spinner dolphins leap and play in the warm waters as the sun paints the sky in brilliant colors.

Underwater Dining

Dine surrounded by the ocean at one of the world unique underwater restaurants, watching marine life swim past as you enjoy gourmet cuisine beneath the waves.

Overwater Spa

Indulge in world-class spa treatments in a pavilion suspended over the ocean, with the soothing sounds of waves providing the perfect natural soundtrack.

Private Sandbank Picnic

Escape to a deserted sandbank in the middle of the ocean for a private champagne picnic, surrounded by nothing but turquoise water and blue sky.

Plan Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

Dry (Nov-Apr)

84F

The northeast monsoon brings sunshine, calm seas, and excellent visibility for diving and snorkeling. Peak season with premium prices.

Peak (Dec-Mar)

86F

The driest months with the best weather. Book well in advance as luxury resorts fill up quickly during this premium period.

Shoulder (Nov, Apr)

85F

Transition months offering good weather with slightly lower prices. Great balance of conditions and value for money.

Wet (May-Oct)

82F

Southwest monsoon brings more rain and stronger winds but also dramatic sunsets, better surfing, and significant resort discounts.

Choose Your Atoll

Research different atolls carefully. North and South Male are closest to the airport while remote atolls offer more pristine reefs and exclusive privacy.

Protect the delicate coral reefs by using only reef-safe, mineral-based sunscreen. Many resorts now require this to preserve the marine environment.
