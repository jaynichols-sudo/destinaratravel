Savannah, GA

Southern Elegance Draped in Spanish Moss

Savannah, Georgia is one of America's most beautiful and romantic cities — a beautifully preserved antebellum gem where moss-draped live oaks arch over cobblestone streets, historic squares invite leisurely strolls, and gracious architecture tells stories of centuries past. From the legendary River Street to the hauntingly beautiful Bonaventure Cemetery, Savannah seduces visitors with its languid Southern charm, world-class cuisine, and rich history.
