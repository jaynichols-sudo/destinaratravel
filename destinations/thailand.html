Thailand

The Land of Smiles — Temples, Beaches & Vibrant Culture

Thailand enchants every traveler who sets foot in its golden temple courtyards, floats through its jungle-fringed river markets, or kicks off their sandals on its powder-white beaches. It's a country of extraordinary contrasts — bustling Bangkok and peaceful Chiang Mai hill tribe villages, turquoise Andaman islands and ancient Sukhothai ruins, fire-breathing street food stalls and serene Buddhist monasteries.

Destination Highlights

Bangkok — Asia's Most Dynamic City

Navigate Bangkok's layers: floating markets at dawn, the gleaming Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew, elevated sky bars at sunset, and legendary night street food on Yaowarat Road.

Chiang Mai & Thai Hill Tribes

Explore northern Thailand's cultural capital — ancient temple circuits, elephant sanctuary encounters, hill tribe village treks, Thai cooking classes, and the unforgettable Yi Peng lantern festival.

Thai Islands — Krabi, Koh Samui & Phi Phi

Sail between dramatic limestone karsts in Phang Nga Bay, snorkel in the crystalline waters of the Similan Islands, and watch Phi Phi's Maya Bay appear from the sea.

Ancient Kingdom of Ayutthaya

Wander the ruined temples and headless Buddhas of Ayutthaya — Thailand's former capital and UNESCO World Heritage Site — just 90 minutes north of Bangkok.

Authentic Thai Cooking Classes

Master pad thai, green curry, tom kha gai, and mango sticky rice with a local chef — from market tours to wok-over-flame cooking in traditional kitchens.

Elephant Conservation Sanctuaries

Spend a day at an ethical elephant sanctuary near Chiang Mai — bathing, feeding, and walking alongside rescued elephants in natural forest settings, not rides or shows.
